My journey as a bootcamp student

After struggling to find in-depth, well organized resources for learning Frontend development, I discovered Scrimba. With Scrimba I have learned the nessecary skills of a Frontend developer and made many projects that show case them.

What I have learned

I have learned that knowing every little thing matters. One should know why they use divs, what makes your website responsive, whats the correct way of building an application. There are many different ways to do these things, but there is a correct way.

Scrimba has taught me how to make resposive and dynamic applications. Applications that fit multiple screen sizes and respond when you interact with them. Integrating JavaScript, to add more functionality, and add a layer to my application that makes them more than static.

Projects I have made

I have made multiple projects some of which include:

A Resturant order application. Allows you to add and remove items from your checkout. Calculate the total of the items in checkout. Buy your items by asking for card information and placing the order.

A password generator application. With the click of a button a javascript program runs and creates a password made of random characters. Users get the option between two generated passwords.
